## RULES FOR EVERY POST

- Write in first person. This is my voice, not a brand voice.
- Do not use corporate language or jargon.
- Do not use urgency language ("act now," "limited time," "don't miss out").
- Do not make claims I cannot back up.
- Every post should feel like something I would actually say out loud.
- Hashtags should be relevant and specific — not generic (#instagood, #content, #lifestyle).
